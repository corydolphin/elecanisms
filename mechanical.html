Meet TankFollower

Meet TankFollower, a versatile, following robot built in six weeks for Elecanisms, an electrical and mechanical prototyping course at Olin College of Engineering.

Our externally facing goal was to design and build, a versatile, autonomous robot that follows a person. Internally, our goal was to continue learning about the integration of mechanical, electrical, and software systems.
